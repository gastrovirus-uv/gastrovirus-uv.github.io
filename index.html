Welcome to the University of Exeter DNA sequencing service’s bioinformatics training resources

These training materials were initiated by the Wellcome Trust Biomedical Informatics Hub, a project that enhanced the University of Exeter’s biomedical research capability by supporting biomedical data analysis.

The Hub was a team of data experts in bioinformatics, metabolomics and proteomics, tomography and image analysis, computing and statistics. The team provided analytical support for biomedical research and a range of training.

These training materials are now maintatined and developed by the University of Exeter’s sequencing team. Visit our website at http://biosciences.exeter.ac.uk/sequencing/.
